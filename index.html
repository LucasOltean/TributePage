Elon Musk timeline and achivements

  • June 28, 1971 - Born in South Africa.
  • Age 12 - Creates and writes a video game called Blastar and sells it for the equivalent of $500.
  • 1989 to 1991 - Attends college at Queens University in Kingston, Ontario. Then transfers to the University of Pennsylvania; completed a BS in Economics (Wharton) and a BA with a major in physics.
  • 1995 - Moves to Silicon Valley.
  • 2000 - Morphs X.com into PayPal.
  • 2002 - Founds SpaceX.
  • 2008 - Becomes Tesla's CEO.
  • June 2012 - Tesla begins deliveries of the all-electric Model S.
  • 2016 - Revealed Mars colonization exploration architecture details.
  • 2018 - Musk and Grimes announced that they were dating.
  • November 2020 -Elon Musk has topped Microsoft founder Bill Gates to become the world's second richest person.
